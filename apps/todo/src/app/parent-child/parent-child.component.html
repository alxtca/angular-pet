Parent-child communication

    Communication through a service
  • Inject and call service method on Parent.
  • Service use BehaviorSubject. Method use next(new value).
  • Child injects service and subscribes to BehaviorSubject
    Communication directly
  • Parent use BehaviorSubject. Method use next(new value).
  • Child takes BehaviorSubject variable as Input() and subscribes to BehaviorSubject
