I want to observ a variable

  1. Make an observable out of it: this.apples$ = of(this.apples)
  2. Subscribe to observable: this.apples$.subscribe(v=> console.log("aplles value: ", v))
  3. And watch observable emit one time. Does not re-emit even if apples value changes.
